A no-bake, glossy layered trifle in patriotic colors — the prettiest (and easiest) 4th of July dessert on the table.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Whip cream, powdered sugar & vanilla to soft peaks
  2. Add a layer of whipped cream to your glass trifle dish
  3. Layer blueberries, then whipped cream, then strawberries
  4. Repeat layers, arranging berries in stripes for the red/white/blue look
  5. Chill 15 minutes, top with mint & extra berries, serve cold
